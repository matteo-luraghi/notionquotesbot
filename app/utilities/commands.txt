start - initialize the bot
quote - send a random quote from the database
author {name} - sends all the quotes from the author
authors - lets you see all the authors and send all the quotes from them
title {book} - sends all the quotes from the chosen book
titles - lets you see all the books and send all the quotes in the chosen one
titleauthor {name} - lets you see all the books from the author and send all the quotes from them
help - see all the commands
